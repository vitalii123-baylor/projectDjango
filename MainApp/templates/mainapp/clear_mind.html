Clear Your Mind

The "Second Brain" concept suggests that your brain is for having ideas, not for holding them. By documenting your learning journey, you free up mental resources for creativity and deep thinking.

Why log your learning?
  • Reduce Anxiety: Getting thoughts out of your head lowers stress levels.
  • Improve Retention: The act of writing reinforces memory.
  • Find Connections: Seeing your notes helps you connect dots between different topics.
